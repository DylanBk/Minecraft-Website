Who are we?

This website was designed and created by Dylan and Jacob. Who are we? We are college students taking a T-Level course on Technical Qualification in Digital Production, Design and Development.


This website was developed as part of a project for our course. The goal was to learn to work within a team to develop core skills such as teamwork and communication. The project itself also further developed technical skills such as website design, UI design, and general programming skills.
