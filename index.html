Welcome to Crafter's Corner!

What is Crafter's Corner?

Crafter's Corner is a home for Minecraft players looking to bring their ideas to life. Here, you can find information on blocks, items, resources and more in our wiki. Need inspiration? Check out our sections on block palettes, builds, map art, and redstone. By signing up, you can also upload your creations for others to see. Want to test your Minecraft knowledge? Try our Mindle game.



Why did we make this website?

Although this website is a college project, we wanted to create a website where Minecraft players can access information about the game, design their next build using block palettes, and view creations from other players. We wanted to create a community for people to learn about the game, design their next in-game project, draw inspiration from other players, and share creations.
